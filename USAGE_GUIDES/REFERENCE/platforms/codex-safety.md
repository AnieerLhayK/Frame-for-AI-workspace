# Codex Loading Safety Notes

These notes cover Codex-specific invocation and projection behavior. Skill
authority remains defined by the manifest and source skill.

## character-generator

- Use authorized corpus only.
- Do not commit private corpus material.
- Do not generate impersonation claims.
- Keep generated skills style-inspired.

## character-maintainer

- Patch narrowly.
- Preserve manual evolution.
- Do not edit generator templates in ordinary maintenance.
- Require a generalization note before generator changes.
- Show diffs before commit.
